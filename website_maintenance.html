Higher security needs (SSL certificates, vulnerability scans, DDoS protection, compliance requirements) add to maintenance costs.

Update Frequency

WordPress sites require regular core, theme, and plugin updates. More frequent updates require more maintenance time.

Cost Impact: Medium

Weekly updates vs monthly updates can increase costs by 30-50%.

Traffic Volume

High-traffic sites require more robust hosting, monitoring, and performance optimization efforts.

Cost Impact: High

High traffic sites (>10k daily visits) cost 2-3x more to maintain.

Content Updates

Cost Impact: Medium
