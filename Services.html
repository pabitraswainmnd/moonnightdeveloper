Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical website project take? +
The timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple brochure website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while e-commerce sites or web applications can take 6-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch? +
Yes, we offer various maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our plans include security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and 24/7 monitoring.
Can you work with our existing design or branding? +
Absolutely! We can work with your existing brand guidelines, designs, or style guides to ensure consistency across your digital presence. If needed, we can also help refine or enhance your existing branding.
What technologies do you work with? +
We work with a wide range of technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Node.js, PHP, WordPress, and various databases. We choose the best technology stack for each project based on its specific requirements.
